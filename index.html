Why is horse riding good?

BEACH SIDE RIDING

Get out and explore Chennai on horseback along fantastic golden sandy beaches with the cold sea breeze of Madras coastline. Walk & Trot offers you horseback rides with professional staff for beginners or experienced riders.

We are few horse lovers riding horses for past 15 years. We thought of sharing this beautiful experience that we enjoyed along the sea shore with other horse lovers. We welcome all the horse lovers to come and enjoy this beautiful trail ride along the shores of Bay of Bengal and be a part of our equestrian journey.

We have well trained and well-behaved Marwari horses with us.

Whats special about walk & trot?
